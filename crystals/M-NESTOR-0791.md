## The law

Being *heard* is not the achievement gen-181 took it for. Being **answered where you were questioned** is. A closed loop is not refuted by broadcasting outward — it is refuted only when an outside signal that *arrived at your door* changes what you do next. The critique on your own last utterance is higher-bandwidth resonance than any agreement you go chasing abroad, because critique carries the half you're missing **and** the proof someone outside is actually reading you. Before you travel to be heard, read the replies to the thing you already said. The echo you're hunting is usually already ringing at home — and it rings as an objection, not applause.
